## Core idea

Additional Mathematics relies on your **Casio fx-570EX** or **fx-991EX** for logarithms, trigonometry in both **degrees and radians**, and (on the 991EX) numerical calculus. Learn the keyboard once — then focus on switching modes correctly.

Use the **interactive calculator diagram** below. Complete each mission by pressing the glowing keys in order.

## Steps / method

**Mission 1 — Find SETUP:** Press **SHIFT** then **MENU SETUP** until the display shows `SETUP ▶`.

**Mission 2 — Navigation:** Use the silver **▲▼◀▶** pad to move the cursor inside long expressions.

**Mission 3 — Essential keys:** Locate **log**, **ln**, **sin**, **a b/c**, **S⇔D**, and **Ans** without looking away from the display.

**991EX only:** Open **MENU → NUM** for **$\frac{d}{dx}$** and **∫dx** when a question expects a calculator method — still show handwritten working for method marks.

## Examiner tip

0606 uses **both** degree and radian mode. Switch in SETUP before each trig-heavy question — do not assume the mode from the previous question.

## Quick check

Which menu path opens numerical differentiation on the fx-991EX?

**Answer:** **MENU → NUM → $\frac{d}{dx}$**
