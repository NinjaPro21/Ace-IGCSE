## Core idea

IGCSE Mathematics 0580 uses **degrees** for almost all trigonometry. Your calculator must show **D** or **Deg** on the status line before you attempt any sin, cos, or tan question.

## Steps / method

**Mission — Degree check:** On the interactive diagram, press **sin → 3 → 0 → =** in order. The display must show **0.5**.

If you see **−0.988…**, you are in **radians** — open **SHIFT + MENU SETUP → Angle Unit → Deg** before continuing.

After clearing memory (**SHIFT + CLR → Setup Data**), re-run the sin 30 test.

## Examiner tip

0580 rarely uses radians. Stay in **Degree** mode for the whole exam unless a question explicitly says radians.

## Quick check

What should **sin 30** equal on your calculator in the correct 0580 mode?

**Answer:** **0.5**
