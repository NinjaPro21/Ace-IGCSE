## Core idea

Before any 0580 paper, know where the essential keys are on your **Casio fx-570EX** or **fx-991EX**. Both models share the same ClassWiz keyboard — muscle memory here prevents lost marks from wrong mode or bad syntax.

Use the **interactive calculator diagram** below. Complete each mission by pressing the glowing keys in order.

## Steps / method

**Mission 1 — Find SETUP:** Press **SHIFT** then **MENU SETUP** on the diagram until the display shows `SETUP ▶`.

**Mission 2 — Navigation:** Use the silver **▲▼◀▶** pad to move the cursor inside long expressions after typing.

**Mission 3 — Fractions:** Press **a b/c** to enter stacked fractions — safer than typing `÷` twice for nested expressions.

**Mission 4 — Essential keys:** Locate **log**, **ln**, **sin**, **S⇔D**, and **Ans** without looking away from the display.

## Examiner tip

Cambridge allows these Casio models in every 0580 paper. You may **not** use a calculator that performs symbolic algebra — stick to the approved ClassWiz.

## Quick check

Which two keys open the SETUP menu?

**Answer:** **SHIFT** (gold key) then **MENU SETUP** (yellow **SETUP** above it).
